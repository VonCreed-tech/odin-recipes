Roasted Vegetable Lasagna

Home

Description

Roasted vegetable lasagna is a vibrant twist on a classic dish, with layers of roasted veggies, creamy ricotta, and mozzarella..

Ingredients

  • 2 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 medium eggplant, cubed
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1/2 medium onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 1/2 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. Prep and Roast the Vegetables: Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Toss the zucchini, eggplant, bell pepper, onion, and garlic with olive oil, Italian seasoning, ½ tsp salt, and ½ tsp black pepper. Spread the vegetables evenly on two baking sheet pans and roast for 25-30 minutes until tender and slightly caramelized.
  2. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture: In a medium bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, eggs, ½ cup Parmesan, 1 cup mozzarella, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp black pepper, basil, and parsley until well combined.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Sheets: Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package directions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread ¼ cup marinara sauce on the bottom of a 13×9 baking pan. Lay 3 lasagna sheets on top. Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by ⅓ of the roasted vegetables. Top with marinara sauce and a sprinkle of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat this process twice, finishing with a final layer of noodles. Top the final layer with marinara sauce and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake the Lasagna: Cover the dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake uncovered for an additional 10-15 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Let It Rest: Allow the lasagna to cool for 25-30 minutes before slicing. This ensures clean layers and an easier serving process.
